    📘 How PhishShield Works

    Read the email. Click anything suspicious (links, buttons, sender, attachments). Each correct click explains why it’s risky.

    When you think you’ve found everything, click Finish Round. You’ll see what you missed.

    Common Tells

    • Urgency & fear ("act now", "your account will be closed")
    • Mismatched sender vs. display name
    • Look‑alike domains (e.g., amaz0n.com)
    • Unexpected attachments (especially .zip/.exe)
    • Requests for passwords or MFA codes

    🛡️ Safety Tips

    • Hover over links to preview the real URL.
    • Check the sender domain carefully. Tiny changes matter.
    • Never enter credentials from an email link; go to the site directly.
    • Enable MFA. Use password managers; prefer long passphrases.
    • When unsure, forward to a trusted person or IT help desk.
